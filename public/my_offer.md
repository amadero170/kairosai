# My Offer — Unified Lead Operations Systems

## English

*(LinkedIn bio, Upwork profile, cold email)*

There's no single view of how many leads are coming in, who's handling them, where they stand in the funnel, or which campaigns are actually working. ### Full offer breakdown
*(proposals, landing page, sales calls)*

> **The problem:** Your leads arrive through WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, email, and your website — but land in different apps, on different phones, managed by different people with no shared visibility. Your CRM is updated manually (if at all), so your pipeline is always outdated. You don't know how many leads you're generating, which campaigns are working, or where in the funnel each prospect is sitting.
>
> **What I build:**
>
> **Visibility & Organization:**
> - Connects all your digital channels (WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, email, web chat) into **one shared inbox**
> - Gives your team a single place to **assign conversations, tag leads, and manage the pipeline** — with full history per contact
> - **Integrates with your CRM** — leads flow in automatically, no manual updates, pipeline always current
> - **Campaign and source analytics** — know exactly where each lead comes from, which channels perform best, and where leads drop off in the funnel
>
> **AI Layer — Instant Response:**
> - A **custom AI chatbot** (not generic — trained on your business) that responds in seconds on first contact, 24/7
> - Qualifies the lead with the right questions and hands it off to the right person only when it's ready
> - Handles after-hours inquiries so no lead goes cold overnight or on weekends
>
> **The result:** Your team has one place to manage every conversation. Your pipeline is always up to date. You know which campaigns generate leads and what happens to them. Every lead gets an instant response, day or night.

This is NOT a one-size-fits-all SaaS — it's a **done-for-you implementation** tailored to each client's specific workflow and channels. Built for SMBs that receive leads through digital channels and operate without visibility over their pipeline.

| Vertical | Core Pain Point | Key Use Case |
